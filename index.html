How to play | Original game



Just like the normal game.


Reward: First Dimension autobuyer
Buying anything halts your production, gradually coming back over 3 minutes.


Reward: Second Dimension autobuyer
First dimension is heavily weakened but gets an exponentially increasing bonus that resets on reset.


Reward: Third Dimension autobuyer
Buying a dimension automatically erases all lower tier dimensions.


Reward: Fourth Dimension autobuyer
Tickspeed starts at 5%.


Reward: Fifth Dimension autobuyer
Each dimension costs the dimension 2 before it, with modified prices.


Reward: Sixth Dimension autobuyer
Multiplier per 10 dimensions is random from 0.30 and 10 (the distribution of multipliers is log-uniform).


Reward: Seventh Dimension autobuyer
There are only eight dimensions, but there's a "Dimension Boost" (much like a shift) every 20 of the current maximum dimension.


Reward: Eighth Dimension autobuyer
Whenever you buy 10 of a dimension, its square increases to the next cost step. Whenever you buy 10 of a square dimension, its square root increases to the next cost step.


Reward: Ninth Dimension autobuyer
To get a Dimension Shift, instead of 20 of a dimension, you need 100.


Reward: Tenth Dimension autobuyer
You can get at most 121 of each dimension.


Reward: Eleventh Dimension autobuyer
Twelve is used instead of ten, ten is used instead of twelve.


Reward: Twelfth Dimension autobuyer
Every minute on average, the game resets (keeping Dimension Shifts).


Reward: 13th Dimension autobuyer
Every 10 minutes on average, the game resets (losing all Dimension Shifts).


Reward: 14th Dimension autobuyer
There's normal matter which rises. If it exceeds your antimatter, it will reset (keeping Dimension Shifts).


Reward: 15th Dimension autobuyer
Every amount of antimatter of the form 2^(2^n) with integer n from 0 to 9 is a fake infinity at which the game must be reset (losing all Dimension Shifts).


Reward: 16th Dimension autobuyer
Whenever you buy 10 of a dimension or buy a tickspeed, everything else of equal cost will increase to its next cost step.


Reward: Tickspeed autobuyer
Dimension Shifts give no multiplier at all.


Reward: Automated Dimension Shifts
There are 16 dimensions from the start, but each dimension produces the dimension 2 below it, and first dimensions produce half the usual antimatter.


Reward: Automated Big Crunches
You first must do 9 challenges of your choice together (other than the first one and this one), and then must do the other 9 together.


Reward: Hyper autobuyer
